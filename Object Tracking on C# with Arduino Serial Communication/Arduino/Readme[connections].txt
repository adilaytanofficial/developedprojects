You have 9 Led and 9 Resistor(330 Ohm)
Your ports: 3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10,11

Top View of Led should be like 3x3 matrix.And start to connect ports
from 3 to 11.

For each port repeat this steps:
	1-) Connect the resistor to port by the help of jumper cable.
	2-) Connect the (+) leg of LED to resistor.
	3-) Connect the (-) leg of LED to GND of Arduino.
